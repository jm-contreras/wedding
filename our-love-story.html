Our Love Story

How It All Began

Some love stories start with a spark. Ours started with a melting ice cream cone.

Morgan and Juanma matched on Hinge and met for their first date on July 3rd, 2024 in Washington, DC, one of the hottest days of the summer, clocking in at a sweltering 100 degrees. They chose ice cream, which turned out to be both a brilliant and terrible idea. For the first stretch of the date, there was barely any talking, just laughing and frantically licking as their cones melted faster than they could keep up. Somehow, it was perfect.

After surviving the heat, Juanma took Morgan on a little detour through DC history, introducing her to two lesser-known gems: the WWI Memorial and the hitching post on New York Avenue outside the Presbyterian church where Abraham Lincoln used to park his horse. From there, the conversation flowed easily into the things that matter most: family, travels, and yes, both of the "forbidden dinner table topics." They covered politics and religion on the first date and lived to tell the tale.

Going Steady

From that first date, things moved naturally and joyfully. They began introducing each other to their worlds, including their music. Morgan brought house and techno; Juanma brought heavy metal and jazz. The moment they knew they were officially a thing came during a trip to New York City, when Juanma tagged along with Morgan and her friends to his very first hard techno night. Something clicked. They've been inseparable ever since.

Adventures Together

If there's one thing Morgan and Juanma share, it's a love of being out in the world together. What started with that first trip to New York City quickly turned into a full passport of adventures: soaking up the sun in Puerto Rico, exploring the breathtaking landscapes of Bolivia, a quick getaway to Boston, dancing in Ibiza, wandering the canals of Amsterdam, and chasing lava and sea turtles on the Big Island of Hawaii. Along the way, they've jumped out of an airplane together, ventured deep underground through caves, and gone snorkeling, because apparently, if there's an adventure on the menu, they're ordering it.

Back home, Juanma has also stepped proudly into the role of dog dad to Morgan's greyhound, Auri, and he's absolutely nailing it.

The Big Question

On their one-year anniversary, Juanma whisked Morgan away to the stunning island of Hydra, Greece. Morgan had no idea what was coming. What she did know was that the day before, Juanma had run the Athens Marathon, all 26.2 miles of it. So when he got down on one knee and asked her to marry him, and she said yes, there was one small logistical issue: his legs had quite literally given out. Morgan had to help her new fiancé back to his feet, knees weak from a combination of emotion and, well, the previous day's marathon. It was romantic, hilarious, and entirely them.

They can't wait to celebrate with the people they love most.
